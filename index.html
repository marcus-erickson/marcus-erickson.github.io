From "It Works!" to Production-Ready

AI is amazing at getting you to 80%. The last 20% is where apps live or die — and most builders don't know what they're missing.

You just built something on Lovable, Bolt, or Cursor in record time. It runs. The features work. You're excited and ready to share it.

Then reality hits.

What catches most vibe-coded apps before they're ready:
  • ⚠️ Exposed API keys — visible in source code to anyone who looks
  • ⚠️ No error boundaries — one bad API call crashes everything
  • ⚠️ Console logs everywhere, leaking data you forgot about
  • ⚠️ Works on your device… your users get a white screen
  • ⚠️ Performance tanks the moment real data loads
  • ⚠️ Accessibility becomes "we'll do it later" — later never comes
  • ⚠️ App Store rejection: missing privacy policy, wrong permissions

The gap between "AI helped me build this" and "this is ready for real users" is bigger than most builders expect. AI gets you fast results — shipping safely takes a checklist.
